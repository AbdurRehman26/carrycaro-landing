CarryCaro

CarryCaro

Free

A platform where people can connect with travellers from any part of the world going to or coming from a city of your desire to send and receive packages.

Features

Explore some of CarryCaro's features

Travel Info

View the traveller's information such as departure and arrival time, location, and more

Delivery Request

View the delivery request details such as package size, weight, and more

Exchange Personal Info

Once approved, your personal information will be exchanged with the traveller
